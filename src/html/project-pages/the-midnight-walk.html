The Midnight Walk

VR game where you walk the midnight walk as the Burnt one alongside Potboy

Tools used:

Unreal Engine 5, Substance Designer, Substance Painter, Material Maker

Project type & Timeframe:

Commercial project (indie studio), 2 years

What does this showcase?

This shows how i can work in a larger team, have previous experience with long term projects and in a professional environment.
I can also use unreal engine and its various systems like Niagara for effects, blueprints for functionality, animation blueprints for setting up animation states and animation graphs. Shader graphs for creating advanced and optimized shaders. All of this while keeping it to the limits and performance budget of a VR environment
