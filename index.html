My name is Elliot Lundgren, I'm a Technical Artist

I have spent almost a decade working with digital visuals both alone and in groups of all sizes. Problem solving is my niche and I approach every task with curiosity and intent on learning something new. I find it very important to learn from the people around you and help them grow aswell.
There is always something new to learn and ways to have fun while doing so, as an example, i have written this entire website by hand using HTML, CSS and Javascript.

since collaboration between roles is important for tech artists, I've studied various programs and tools to learn how and when they can be used for areas both within and outside the role of a tech artist because i find being able to work with anyone is very valuable.

In my free time I enjoy playing both digital and physical games, create stuff in various forms like shaders, electronics, music, programming, cooking, drawing and clay figures just to name a few.
