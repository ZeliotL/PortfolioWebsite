My name is Elliot Lundgren, I'm a Technical Artist

I have spent almost a decade working with digital visuals both individually and cooperatively. I thrive on tackling challenges and I approach every task with curiosity and intent on learning something new.

Considering collaboration between roles is important for tech artists, I've studied various programs and tools to learn how and when they can be used for in varied areas. I strive to learn from the people around me and help them grow aswell.

I have enjoyed trying out all sorts of creative hobbies since I was young. Creating things in many mediums and playing both digital and physical games is something I frequently enjoy doing to this day.
